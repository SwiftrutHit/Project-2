How to source Indian handicraft
products in 2023

Musharif City
14 March, 2020

India is a country that is known for its rich cultural heritage and artistic skills. One of the many things that India is famous for is its handicraft products.

Handicrafts have been a part of India's rich cultural history for centuries, and there are many artisans and craftsmen across the country who are still continuing this tradition today. If you are interested in sourcing Indian handicraft products, here are some of the steps you can follow:

  1. 1. Research the type of products and find your interest

    The first step is to research the different types of handicraft products that are available in India. India has a rich diversity of handicrafts, from hand-woven textiles to intricate jewelry, pottery and woodwork. You should also research the different regions in India that are known for specific handicrafts. For example, Jaipur is known for its block-printed textiles, while Agra is famous for its marble inlay work. We have mentioned the region wise specialty of Indian handicraft products ahead in this article.

  2. 2. Identify and shortlist suppliers

    Once you have a good idea of the types of handicraft products you want to source, you can start identifying suppliers. One of the best ways to find suppliers is to attend trade shows and exhibitions that showcase Indian handicrafts. This will give you the opportunity to meet with suppliers face-to-face, see their products up close, and have an understanding of the tradition. You can also search online on B2B platforms, trade portals, export promotion council directories for suppliers and manufacturers of Indian handicrafts. We have mentioned more about this ahead in this article.

  3. 3. Check for authenticity

    When sourcing handicrafts from India, it is important to ensure that the products are authentic and not counterfeit. Look for suppliers who are registered with the government and have the necessary certifications and licenses. You should also ask for samples of the products before placing a bulk order to ensure that the quality of the products meets your standards.
